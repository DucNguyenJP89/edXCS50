Available Courses

Basic Course

Basic course will focus on 4 skills of Japanese at basic level, using みんなの日本語 and まるごと textbooks.

  • Vocabulary: みんなの日本語, other materials
  • Grammar: まるごとA1-A2, other materials
  • Reading: みんなの日本語
  • Listening: まるごとA1-A2 かつどう

JLPT Courses

JLPT courses will focus on practising to improve your vocabulary and reading skill so that you can get better result in the test.
The course will use different textbooks for each section.

  • Vocabulary&Expressions: 耳から覚える語彙, other materials
  • Reading: スピードマスター読解, other materials
  • Listening: 完全マスター読解, other materials
